Elegant Styling

On the other hand, we denounce with righteous indignation and dislike men who are so beguiled and demoralized by the charms of pleasure of the moment, so blinded by desire, that they cannot foresee the pain and trouble that are bound to ensue. Equal blame belongs to those who fail in their duty through weakness of will. The wise man therefore always holds in these matters to this principle of selection: he rejects pleasures to secure other greater pleasures or endures pains to avoid worse pains.
